# Weekly Meals Logo

Primary direction: a simple monogram-based `W`.

Why this works:
- It feels more like a platform/app brand than a literal food icon.
- It stays readable as a tiny app icon and as a larger brand mark.
- It can live in one color, similar to simple modern marks like Uber or X.

Files:
- `weekly-meals-logo-icon.svg`: rounded app-icon style version with dark base and blue/cyan glow.
- `weekly-meals-logo-mark.svg`: clean standalone mark for monochrome usage.

Design intent:
- `W` = Weekly / week rhythm.
- Soft rounded stroke = food/lifestyle tone without becoming childish.
- Blue/cyan glow = matches the current app palette and liquid dashboard look.
